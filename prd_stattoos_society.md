# Project PRD: STATTOOS SOCIETY Digital Presence

## 1. Project Overview
**Brand Name:** STATTOOS SOCIETY
**Tagline:** "MAKING FRIENDS NO CLIENTS"
**Industry:** High-end Tattoo & Body Modification Studio
**Core Philosophy:** A sanctuary of technical excellence meeting an uncompromising underground aesthetic. Focus on community ("friends") over transactional relationships ("clients").

## 2. Visual Identity & Design System
**Theme Name:** Cinematic Noir / Monochrome Industrial
**Color Palette:** 
- Primary: Deep Charcoal / Pure Black (`#000000`, `#131313`)
- Accents: Metallic Gold, Brushed Silver
**Typography:** 
- Headlines: Bold, heavy sans-serif (Inter / Anybody) for a cinematic, high-impact feel.
- Body: Clean, readable sans-serif.
**UI Style:** 
- Glassmorphism for navigation (Backdrop blur + semi-transparent overlays).
- High-contrast, black-and-white imagery.
- Cinematic video backgrounds.
- Minimalist navigation and clean grid layouts.

## 3. Site Map & Navigation
**Header (Common):** Glassmorphism style with the official STATTOOS SOCIETY logo (SVG), navigation links, and a primary "BOOK NOW" CTA.
**Footer (Common):** Studio philosophy, social links, legal info, and location details.

### 3.1 Home (Inicio)
- **Hero Section:** Full-screen cinematic video background. Minimalist overlay with tagline "MAKING FRIENDS NO CLIENTS".
- **Artist Preview:** Introduction to the master artists.
- **Studio Ethos:** Narrative section detailing the studio's history and philosophy.
- **Lead Gen:** Section for tattoo/body mod quotes with a direct contact CTA.

### 3.2 Artists (Artistas)
- **Gallery:** High-impact, large-scale imagery of resident artists.
- **Filtering:** Ability to filter by style (Neotraditional, Realism, etc.).

### 3.3 Artist Profile (Perfil de Artista)
- **Header:** Artist specific hero with name, specialization, and years of experience.
- **Portfolio:** Mixed media grid showing finished works and process videos.

### 3.4 Conventions (Convenciones)
- **Narrative Scroll:** A chronological portfolio of the last 5 major events.
- **Content:** Large video headers for each event, followed by a short description and photo gallery.

### 3.5 Piercings
- **Portfolio:** Dedicated space for body modifications and high-end piercings.
- **Ethos:** Focus on technical mastery and medical-grade sterility.

### 3.6 Booking
- **Contact:** Integration with WhatsApp Concierge for immediate response.
- **Location:** Interactive map and "Studio Rituals" (Hours of operation).
- **CTA:** Online booking and email inquiry options.

## 4. Technical Requirements
- **Responsive Design:** Fully optimized for Desktop and Mobile (Portrait).
- **High-Performance Video:** Efficient loading of video backgrounds and portfolio clips.
- **Asset Preservation:** Use of the specific STATTOOS SOCIETY SVG logo in all headers.
- **Interactivity:** Smooth scroll transitions between event sections in the Conventions page.

## 5. Content Strategy
- **Tone of Voice:** Sophisticated, exclusive, professional, and slightly underground.
- **Language:** Spanish (primary) with English brand elements.
- **Imagery:** Professional photography with consistent desaturation or monochrome treatment.
